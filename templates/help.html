In-Battle
eDPS → Effective Damage Per Second

Effective Damage per Second means the amount of effective damage the pokemon does every second when spamming. If it is super effective on the enemy (Depending on the typings), it will do more damage. For example, Mega Charizard Y might do a ton of damage to Mamoswine per attack. If it is not very effective (Usually when the typing of the attack is the same as the one you're attacking or the weakness is attacking the strength), it won't really do much damage.

% → Strength Comparision

The strength comparision (The one with the %), is the amount of IV's for that pokemon. Example, 100% is a hundo or 0% is a nundo. This can basically tell you how strong the pokemon is. 0-stars = Probably not that strong. 1-star = Not very strong. 2-star = Mid strong. 3-star = Strong. 4-star (HUNDO) = Super strong!
